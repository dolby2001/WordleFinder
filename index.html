HOW IT WORKS

I have developed an algorithm that employs the entropy formula to calculate the probabilities associated with 2,309 words selected from a predefined word bank, with the objective of predicting their likelihood of being the correct answer in a game of Wordle. The algorithm takes into account the color indicators of each square, distinguishing between yellow, gray, and green. I employed web scraping, specifically utilizing Selenium, to write my guesses and find the right answer.
